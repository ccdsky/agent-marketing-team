## Production Workflow

**Batching approach:** Create LinkedIn posts in batches (2 weeks at a time). Write newsletters week-by-week for freshness. Blog posts can be done 2 weeks ahead.

**Quality threshold for publishing:**
- LinkedIn: 8/10 voice match, strong hook
- Newsletter: 8/10 voice match, one clear idea, concrete example
- Blog: 7/10 voice match, SEO elements complete, genuinely useful
