# Product Launch Campaign Template

Use for launching a new product, service, cohort, or major feature. Structured around a pre-launch warming period → launch day → post-launch follow-up.

Fill out and copy to `output/campaigns/[slug]/campaign-brief.md`.

## Pre-Launch Phase (Warming)

**Goal:** Build anticipation and prime the existing audience before launch day.

**Pre-launch duration:** [X] days before launch

**Pre-launch content plan:**

| Day | Channel | Content | Goal |
|-----|---------|---------|------|
| -[X] | [channel] | [Tease or problem content] | Plant the seed |
| -[X] | [channel] | [Behind the scenes / story] | Build connection |
| -[X] | [channel] | [Social proof / case study] | Build credibility |
| -1 | [channel] | ["Tomorrow..." announcement] | Create urgency |

**Pre-launch emails:**
- Email 1 (Day -X): [Topic — usually problem or story]
- Email 2 (Day -X): [Topic — usually solution or preview]
- Email 3 (Day -1): ["It's happening tomorrow" — open loop]

## Launch Day

**Launch time:** [Time + timezone]

**Launch day content:**

| Time | Channel | Content |
|------|---------|---------|
| [time] | Email | Launch announcement |
| [time] | LinkedIn | Launch post |
| [time] | Twitter/X | Launch thread |
| [+2h] | Stories/Notes | Quick reminder |

**Launch email structure:**
1. It's here — deliver the promise from pre-launch
2. What it is, who it's for, what it does
3. Proof (testimonial, case study, or early result)
4. CTA — one link, one action
5. Urgency — if genuine (limited spots, deadline)

| Day | Email Purpose |
